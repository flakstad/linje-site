Pricing

Simple usage pricing.

Pricing is based on messages processed each month. One outbound email is one message. One inbound email delivered to your webhook is one message.

Plan Monthly usage Price
Starter 500 messages / month Free
Growth 5,000 messages / month $5 / month
Scale 50,000 messages / month $25 / month

Counting

Messages are counted in both directions.

Outbound

Each transactional email submitted through Linje counts as one message.

Inbound

Each inbound email delivered to a webhook counts as one message.

Replay

Replay is for recovery and debugging, not a separate workflow product.
